03
Service Modules

Comprehensive Platform
Capabilities

Each module is designed to work independently while integrating seamlessly into a unified platform. All services include real-time reporting, wallet management, and role-based access control.

💳
PG
Payment Gateway
End-to-end payment collection and processing engine supporting multiple payment modes with real-time settlement and reconciliation.
  • Multi-mode payment acceptance (UPI, Cards, Net Banking, Wallets)
  • Real-time webhook notifications & transaction tracking
  • Automated T+1 settlement engine
  • Merchant onboarding & KYC verification
  • MDR/scheme-based charge management
  • Detailed transaction reports & analytics
🖥️
POS
Point of Sale
Complete POS machine lifecycle management — from procurement and inventory to assignment, transactions, rental billing, and settlement.
  • POS machine inventory & bulk upload
  • Machine assignment to merchants/retailers
  • POS Bridge for live device operations (pay, refund, void)
  • Rental subscription management with auto-debit
  • Multi-mode support: Card, UPI, BharatQR, Cash
  • T+1 automated settlement with commission splits
📱
QR
QR Code Payments
Standalone QR-based payment acceptance module enabling merchants to receive payments instantly through static and dynamic QR codes.
  • Static QR generation linked to merchant UPI VPA
  • Dynamic QR with pre-filled amounts for invoicing
  • Real-time payment notification & confirmation
  • Merchant QR dashboard with transaction history
  • QR-based settlement & reconciliation reports
  • Branded QR with merchant details
🏢
MDS
Master Distributor
Top-tier distribution management with full visibility into the downstream hierarchy — distributors, retailers, wallets, and commissions.
  • Distributor onboarding & management
  • Wallet operations (fund transfer, balance management)
  • Scheme & commission structure configuration
  • Full hierarchy transaction reports
  • Service activation/deactivation for downstream
  • Performance analytics & dashboard
🏪
DS
Distributor
Mid-tier distribution portal enabling distributors to manage their retailer network, monitor transactions, and control fund flows.
  • Retailer onboarding & KYC verification
  • Wallet top-up & fund transfers to retailers
  • Commission tracking & earning reports
  • Retailer-wise transaction monitoring
  • Service management for downstream retailers
  • Settlement & reconciliation reports
👤
Retailer
Retailer Portal
Feature-rich retailer dashboard providing access to all enabled payment services with wallet management and transaction history.
  • Access to BBPS, AEPS, DMT, Recharge & other services
  • Digital wallet with real-time balance
  • Transaction history with receipt generation
  • Mini-statement & balance enquiry
  • Dispute filing & resolution tracking
  • Profile & KYC management
🛡️
Sub-Admin
Sub-Administrator Panel
Granular, department-scoped administrative access allowing your team to manage specific operations without full admin privileges.
  • Role-based permission system (wallet, MDR, reports, etc.)
  • Department-scoped access control
  • User management within assigned scope
  • Activity logging & audit trail
  • Reversal & dispute handling (if permitted)
  • Custom report access per department

05
Timeline

Implementation
Roadmap

A phased delivery approach across an estimated 6–8 weeks ensures each module is thoroughly built, tested, and integrated before moving to the next. This reduces risk and allows for feedback at every stage.

Phase 1
Foundation & Admin Core
Weeks 1–2
Project setup, database architecture, authentication system, admin panel with super admin & sub-admin roles, user management, and portal settings. Wallet & ledger system foundation.
Phase 2
Distribution Hierarchy (MDS, DS, Retailer)
Weeks 3–4
Master Distributor dashboard, Distributor portal, Retailer onboarding & management. Wallet fund transfers, commission/scheme engine, and hierarchy-based reporting.
Phase 3
Payment Services (PG, POS, QR)
Weeks 5–6
Payment Gateway integration with multi-mode support, POS machine management with rental & subscription, QR code payment module with static/dynamic QR generation, settlement engine, and MDR configuration.
Phase 4
Testing, Polish & Deployment
Weeks 7–8
End-to-end testing, security audit, performance optimization, reporting finalization, UAT with your team, production deployment on cloud infrastructure, and handover with documentation.
